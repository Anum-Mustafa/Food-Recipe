Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Instruction

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour one 9x13 inch pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, stir together sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda,and salt until there are no visible clumps.
  4. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat with a mixer on medium speed for two minutes.
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan.
  6. Stir in boiling water.
  7. Spoon batter evenly into cupcake wells. Bake for 20 to 22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Remove from oven and let cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then remove from pan and let cool on a rack.
  9. Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to three days, or in the refrigerator for up to a week.
